TableMerger

TableMerger is a table comparer that allows to combine in a single matrix features information held in different datasets, for instance: putative annotations results and experimental or statistical information (e.g. peak area, RT, p-values, among others).

Therefore, TableMerger receives as input two tables specified by the user. The equivalent features of both tables will be identified thanks to their mass and their retention time (if the retention time is not present, only the mass will be considered).
